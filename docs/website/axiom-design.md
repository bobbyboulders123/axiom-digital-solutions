# Axiom Digital Solutions — Source of Truth

## 1. Brand

**Brand name:** Axiom Digital Solutions  
**One-line purpose:** Axiom Digital Solutions builds premium websites and digital experiences for businesses that want to stand out with clarity, quality, and modern design.

## 2. Positioning

Axiom Digital Solutions should present as a premium digital solutions company with a strong technology backbone.

The brand should feel:
- premium
- modern
- intelligent
- high-tech
- credible
- polished
- visually impressive
- conversion-focused

Axiom is not a trendy startup gimmick, a gamer brand, or an experimental art project. It should feel like a company that understands both design quality and technical execution.

## 3. Primary Design Direction

Use a **dark premium tech** aesthetic.

The visual world should be inspired by:
- premium hardware design
- motherboard and circuit-board imagery
- processors and chipsets
- technical infrastructure
- modern software systems
- polished control interfaces
- enterprise-grade digital products

The result should feel like a blend of:
- premium website agency
- modern software company
- digital systems consultancy
- luxury technology brand

## 4. Visual Inspiration

Preferred visual material:
- close-up motherboard photography
- circuit traces and PCB patterns
- processor and chip imagery
- server infrastructure
- refined dashboard/interface overlays
- technical macro photography
- dark hardware/software composites

Use imagery in a refined, cinematic way.

Imagery should be:
- high quality
- darkened for readability
- color-graded to match the brand palette
- integrated into the composition
- used with restraint

## 5. Avoid

Do not make the site feel:
- cosmic
- celestial
- cyberpunk
- gamer-themed
- neon overload
- generic SaaS template
- brutalist
- cartoonish
- cluttered
- gimmicky

## 6. Color System

Base the palette on the Axiom symbol logo.

### Core colors
- Carbon `#0B0F14`
- Midnight `#121A24`
- Graphite `#1B2430`
- Steel `#7C8A99`
- Electric Blue `#2F80ED`
- Cyan `#35D0FF`
- White `#F5F7FA`

### Usage guidance
- Use dark surfaces as the foundation.
- Use Electric Blue and Cyan for highlights, gradients, and emphasis.
- Keep accent usage restrained and premium.
- Prefer subtle glow, polished edges, and elegant line work over loud effects.

## 7. Typography

Typography should feel:
- sharp
- premium
- modern
- highly readable
- technical without looking cold

### Recommended font direction
- **Headings:** Inter, Sora, Outfit, or Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body:** Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Data / UI accents:** IBM Plex Mono or JetBrains Mono

### Typography rules
- Headings should be confident and clean.
- Body text should remain simple and polished.
- Avoid novelty sci-fi fonts.
- Use monospace only for supporting technical accents, labels, or system details.

## 8. Brand Asset Rules

### Logo usage
Use the **bare Axiom symbol logo only** as the uploaded visual brand asset.

Do not rely on a fixed uploaded wordmark lockup.

The builder may typeset the brand name `Axiom Digital Solutions` in a way that best fits the design system.

### Logo placement
Use the symbol logo for:
- navbar
- favicon
- footer
- subtle decorative brand moments

Use typeset brand text alongside it where appropriate.

## 9. Layout Principles

The site should feel structured, intentional, and custom-designed.

### Layout traits
- strong visual hierarchy
- generous spacing
- cinematic hero section
- premium card systems
- clean section rhythm
- polished grids
- elegant transitions
- balanced mix of imagery and copy

Avoid layouts that feel cramped, noisy, or overly experimental.

## 10. Motion Principles

Motion should support the premium feel, not distract from it.

### Include
- smooth reveal animations
- subtle parallax where appropriate
- polished hover transitions
- restrained glow interactions
- refined card movement
- elegant scroll pacing

### Avoid
- chaotic movement
- flashy gimmicks
- excessive motion density
- distortion-heavy effects
- anything that harms readability
